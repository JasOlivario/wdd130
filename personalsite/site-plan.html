About

What is an Amazon Seller Virtual Assistants?

An Amazon Seller VA specializes on tasks that are related to Amazon business. Those tasks include, but are not limited to, Product Research, Souplier Sourcing, Product Listing, PPC (Pay Per Click) Campaign Management, Graphic Design, and Web Development.

Why do you need to Hire an Amazon VA expert?

Amazon Seller Virtual Assistants know almost all the tasks, and they niche down and become experts to just one niche. Hiring an Amazon VA who focus on their chosen expertise saves you time on training new hires. They can help you oversee the work in their department.
